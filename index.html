Features

uRyde connects college students who need transportation. Whether you are offering or requesting rides, uRyde makes it easy to find your way home.

Offers

Making offers provides students a friendly ride home and a great opportunity to network with you while attending university.

Requests

Request a ride from another student heading in the same direction. Posting a request allows students to let you know when they are going home, and are willing to give you a ride.

Notifications

Receive notifications from other students who make offers or request rides from you. Upon acceptance, you are instantly connected with them in order to negotiate.
